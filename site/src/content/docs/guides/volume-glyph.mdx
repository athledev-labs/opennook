---
title: Volume glyph
description: An ambient volume indicator in the compact pill.
---

`SystemVolumeObserver` tracks the default output device's volume and mute
state via public CoreAudio APIs. `NookVolumeIndicator` renders it as a
compact-slot glyph. It shows the level — it does not intercept or replace
Apple's volume HUD. It lives in the `NookComponents` product.

See `Examples/VolumeNook/main.swift` in the repo for the working pattern.
