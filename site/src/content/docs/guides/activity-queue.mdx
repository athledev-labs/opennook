---
title: Activity queue
description: A priority live-activity queue for the expanded surface.
---

`NookActivityQueue` collects transient activities, orders them by priority,
coalesces duplicates, and presents each by briefly taking over the expanded
surface. It yields the surface whenever the user is engaging it. It lives
in the `NookComponents` product. Bind it via `NookConfiguration.onReady`
and render with `NookActivityHost`.

See `Examples/ActivityNook/main.swift` in the repo for the working pattern.
